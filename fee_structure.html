Basic Fee Structure

MOTORCYCLE TRAINING FEES

Novice/Beginner to K53 Test Preparation is charged at the rate of R350.00 per hour or R650.00 per 2 hour lesson.

Basic Handling Skills Course : is charged at R350.00 per hour. We supply all motorcycles and helmets for use during the lesson.

There is no difference in the fee if you use your own motorcycle and equipment for any of our courses

Handling Skills Courses (Intermediate and Advanced) are charged at R350.00 per hour. We recommend using your own motorcycle and helmet for these lessons to give you the benefit of learning to handle the bike you intend to ride.

You could use our bike if required by pre-arrangement.

Road Skills Courses at levels Basic, Intermediate and Advanced are charged at R350.00 per hour.

We supply required communication equipment, you supply the motorcycle and helmet for use during the lesson.

Motorcycle Rental Fee

Use of our bike for the Official K53 test costs R 350.00 if the test is conducted at the Cape Town, Milnerton, Parow or Bellville DLTC s'.

Should you prefer to use your own motorcycle and helmet, for beginners to K53 test prep, you may do so.

There is however, no difference in the fee if you use your own equipment.

PLEASE NOTE : Should your test be booked at a testing station other than those mentioned above then please discuss the charges with our booking office.

You are advised to check with the DLTC if tests will continue in inclement weather conditions.

While our personal (1:1) training for handling skills and road skills is set at the fixed costs listed above, we do have group training sessions which are significantly discounted from time to time. Please enquire about any discounts applicable when you book any group training session.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Cancellation of lessons must be done on 072 175 1985 between 08:00 and 18:00 at least 24 hours prior to scheduled time of lesson.
Cancellation of hours booked, for whatever reason, less than 24 hours prior to the scheduled time will be biled at full cost.

RAIN WEATHER

We train in the rain!
You are advised to bring appropriate rain wear on rainy days.
Lessons scheduled for days on which it rains will continue as scheduled unless our instructors deem conditions unsafe to continue.

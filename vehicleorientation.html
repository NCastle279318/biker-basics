Vehicle Orientation

VEHICLE ORIENTATION is for the rider making the transition from one bike to another. It is for the rider who feels a little unsure of his ability to handle/control the new machine and who needs some assistance to gain confidence/competence in a safe and controlled environment.

It is a course where all the common errors riders on newer/different (to what they are accustomed to) machines are prone to making can be explored and prevented....but without the attendant damage or repair costs. This is to get comfortable with your new purchase course.

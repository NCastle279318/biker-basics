Beyond the Basics

Course Duration: 1/2 day - 4 hours - This course is the bridge between our basic and intermediate level skills development. It is available as individual attention or as a group class depending on your preference. This covers tight turns, slaloms both linear and offset, hand and leg placement for full lock, full and half clutch turns and provides an introduction to negotiating cone maze exercises.

It covers transitions from left to right turns within confined spaces. It goes on to cover the basics of safely completing low to moderate speed corners without dropping acceleration. It ensures that you can execute a safe and balanced U-Turn in a normal road width. You will start to practice some of the exercises required for and comprising the Intermediate Handling Skills Course.

Returning Rider

Course duration - 1/2day -4 hrs - The Returning Rider is the previously licenced rider who, because of personal circumstances, studies, marriage, the birth of the children or work obligations has had to stay off the bike for some time.

This rider now wants to get back into riding, but needs a refresher or confidence building session to ensure they return to their previous riding form.

Regardless of the length of time you have been off the bike, it is recommended that you take some form of structured training to allow you to get into the swing of things in safety and under controlled conditions to ensure the minimum risk to you and to others.

We have carefully structured this course to get rid of the rust that may have started to form and to ensure that you are abreast of the current riding requirements while gently checking for the habits that prove to be a liability..
